# Game Balance

## Early Loop

- Beginner dungeon: level 1-10, 7-10 rooms per run.
- Average normal monster kill: 2-4 turns.
- Boss kill target: 5-8 turns with at least one potion.
- First level-up target: 2-3 normal monster kills.

## Rarity Rates

- Common: baseline 42%.
- Uncommon: baseline 30%.
- Rare: baseline 18%.
- Epic: baseline 7.6%.
- Legendary: baseline 2%.
- Mythic: baseline 0.4%.

Luck shifts loot rolls by up to 12%. Reincarnation increases high-rarity job rates, capped to prevent runaway inflation.

## Enhancement

- +1 to +10: 100% success.
- +11 onward: starts near 86%, then drops by roughly 7% per level.
- MVP failure does not destroy gear. Beta can add downgrade tickets and protection stones.

## Reincarnation

- Unlocks at level 100.
- Resets level to 1.
- Adds reincarnation scaling to base stats.
- Adds one retained trait.
- Improves hidden job discovery odds.
